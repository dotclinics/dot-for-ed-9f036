Our Specialized Services

Hair Transplant (FUE)

Restore hair on bald areas using advanced FUE technique (Follicular Unit Extraction). Follicles are taken from the back of the head and placed on the front, bald areas, beard, mustache, and eyebrows.

Skin Whitening

Glutathione injections used with Vitamin C for complete body whitening and skin brightening. Safe and effective treatments for achieving a radiant complexion.

Acne & Scar Treatment

Comprehensive acne scar treatment using customized PRP, threads, and advanced medications tailored to your skin type.

Pigmentation Treatment

Effective treatment for dark spots, melasma, and freckles caused by sun exposure or steroid-based creams. Restore your natural skin tone.

Advanced treatments for alopecia caused by genetics, hormonal changes, medical conditions, or medications. Hair PRP is the best way to stop hair fall and regrow hair.

Dark Circles Treatment

Treat dark circles caused by fatigue, lack of sleep, genes, dehydration, sun exposure, and allergies with our specialized treatments.

Safe removal of moles, warts (caused by HPV), and skin tags using advanced techniques.

Open Pores Treatment

Minimize open pores caused by sun exposure, genetics, age, hormonal changes, smoking, and poor diet.
